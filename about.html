Professional Bio

Benard Nashon Otieno is a seasoned public policy, research, and data analytics professional with over 10 years of experience supporting government institutions, universities, development partners, and private-sector stakeholders across Africa. His work focuses on strengthening institutional systems, driving evidence-based decision-making, and advancing good governance through robust data and research.

Benard currently serves as the Administrative Officer for Research & Technology Development at the Technical University of Kenya, where he supports scientific and ethics review, research administration, and grant oversight. He is the Secretary General of the Public Policy Professionals Society of Kenya (PPPSK) and has led regional projects with AfDB and AUDA-NEPAD.

  • MA Public Policy & Administration — Kenyatta University (2019)
  • BSc Agricultural Education & Extension — University of Nairobi (2013)
